About It

La Tana

For one evening on May 7, the entrance of the Triennale Teatro was transformed into the dark and warm interior of a den, marked by immense, distant and indistinguishable temporalities. Its corridors have been labyrinthine tunnels, where collective rituals have been animated by diverse and extreme species who have found refuge in it, and who have made the depths of its walls resonate with an echo of voices, sounds and bodies.

The Den was born at the invitation of Triennale Teatro, which entrusted a carte blanche to two artists, Annamaria Ajmone and Silvia Costa, as the closing event of the FOG 2024 Festival. The two artists opened the invitation to ten artists with the idea of creating a dramaturgy composed of fragments of the works of each of them: an original performance built from non-original pieces, but already edited by the authors themselves, which, seen as a whole, are re-signified.

The works of Soukaine Abrour, Benni Bosetto, Lara Dâmaso, Francesca Flora e nobile, Heith, Sara Leghissa, Giulia Polenta, Nicolò Porcelluzzi, Nicola Ratti, Michele Rizzo, Thyself Agency, Ultimabaret, alternate and overlap in the space of the Triennale theater, which is used by overturning its conventions and highlighting its contradictions.

If at first the audience was invited to sit in the seats and enjoy the space in a canonical way, slowly and over the course of the evening, stage space and audience space became indistinguishable: the performances in the stalls, in the gallery, the audience on the stage, in the rehearsal room, in the foyer. In this great disorder, even the masks of the theater became agents of the stage, containing and moving the audience.

The evening opened with Thyself Agency, a travel agency offering a triptych of existential exercises to see oneself differently: the exchange of lives, the nine-day week, and glasses to see upside down.

Thus began the journey into darkness.

Tàna, s. f. “spazio buio scavato per perdersi, spec. terra o cielo”. Fig. “gabbia di respiri”. Casa radice, impronta negativa. Cfr: “Il linguaggio, tana della mente” [assenza di luce, luce ovunque].

Curated by Annamaria Ajmone and Silvia Costa.
With Annamaria Ajmone, Ultimabaret, Benni Bosetto, Francesca Flora Heith, Lara Dâmaso, Thyself Agency, Michele Rizzo, Nicola Ratti, Sara Leghissa, Silvia Costa, Soukaina Abrour.
Graphic design by Giulia Polenta.
Sonnet by Nicolò Porcelluzzi.
Production l'Altra Associazione, Triennale Milano Teatro.

Program

Vieni entra nella tana in controluce

scivolando all'insù per quell'imbuto
dove la porta radica nel muro
natura morta, e canta e pure cuce

tra ceste di cristalli ossa e bucce
di cedri e madreperla e anche un pennuto
che occhieggiando e sbavando chiede aiuto.
Non basta. Più su ancora, lì conduce

quella speranza cieca e cruda fede
nella nuda fatica della mente:
ecco i talpini che studiano insieme

"Quando nasce, la talpa è trasparente
Solo scavando trova il vecchio seme…".
Vive al buio perché non gli fa niente.

Sonnet by Nicolò Porcelluzzi
